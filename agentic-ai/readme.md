Senior DE/DS with AI capability.

## Why Agentic AI

After 2+ years in airline revenue management analytics
and 6 months building production DE infrastructure,
the natural next layer is AI systems that act —
not just predict.

Agentic AI sits at the intersection of:
- Data Engineering (pipelines that feed agents)
- Data Science (models that power agents)
- Software Engineering (systems that deploy agents)

This combination — domain expertise + DE depth +
agentic AI — is what the market will pay a premium
for in 2027 and beyond.
